Ceremonial Bowl

The Ceremonial Bowl is an essential cultural item used in traditional rituals and ceremonies, particularly in Borneo. Typically crafted from materials like wood, bamboo, or clay, these bowls are often decorated with symbolic carvings or designs. They are used to hold offerings, food, or liquids during ceremonies, connecting the physical and spiritual realms. The ceremonial bowl plays a significant role in rituals, such as those for blessings, rites of passage, or festivals.
Beyond its functional purpose, the Ceremonial Bowl represents the community's beliefs and traditions. Its decorations often feature symbols of fertility, prosperity, and protection, reflecting the spiritual values of the culture. The bowl is typically used communally, emphasizing unity and the connection between individuals and their ancestors. Through its use in rituals, the bowl preserves cultural heritage and honors the spiritual practices of the community.l
