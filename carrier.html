Punan Ba'a Baby Carrier

The Punan Ba'a baby carrier is a significant cultural artifact from the indigenous Punan community of Borneo. It is intricately crafted, blending function and artistry.
The carriers often feature detailed carvings, beadwork, and symbolic motifs representing protection, fertility, and the spiritual connection between mother and child.
Materials like wood, beads, and rattan are commonly used, showcasing the community's deep connection to nature and craftsmanship.
These carriers not only serve a practical purpose but also embody the rich traditions and artistic heritage of the Punan people.

Jalan Tun Abang Haji Openg, 93400 Kuching, Sarawak.
