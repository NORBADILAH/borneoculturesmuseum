Director Sarawak Museum

A very warm greeting and salam Ibu Pertiwiku

I would like to wish all of you a warm welcome to our official website of Borneo Cultures Musem and hopefully that this website is able to provide you with the necessary information regarding Borneo Cultures Museum as a whole.

Besides that, this website serves as a platform for Borneo Cultures Musem to share the incoming activities and also the activities that had been carried out for this year. I hope that this website will able to foster the love for Sarawak`s heritage and at the same time to attract more visitors from all over the state and also from foreign countries to visit our museums.

Last but not least, I hope that all of the visitors will able to gain useful information throughout this website. I would like to welcome for any suggestions and positive feedback in order to improve the contents and the information in our website for the benefit of everyone.


Thank you for visiting Borneo Cultures Museum website.

“AN HONOUR TO SERVE”

NANCY HAJI JOLHI
Director
Sarawak Museum Department
