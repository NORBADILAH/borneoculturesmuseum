Art of the Ancestors

The "Art of the Ancestors" encompasses the traditional art of indigenous cultures, reflecting their spiritual beliefs, social structures, and daily life. In Borneo, this includes ceremonial objects, wood carvings, and artworks that hold cultural and ritual significance. These pieces connect to the ancestors' views on nature, the spiritual realm, and community identity.
