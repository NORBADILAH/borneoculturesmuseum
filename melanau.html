Sun Hat | Melanau Peoples

The Sun Hat | Melanau Peoples is a traditional headwear worn by the Melanau people, an indigenous group from the coastal regions of Sarawak, Malaysia. Made from natural materials such as rattan, palm leaves, or other local plant fibers, the sun hat features a wide brim that provides protection from the harsh tropical sun. It is an essential item for the Melanau, particularly during outdoor activities like farming, fishing, and other labor-intensive tasks.
